## The Mission: What is QBronze?

QBronze is the foundational "quantum entangling" workshop hosted by **QWorld**. It is an intensive, task-based program where participants move from the basics of classical probabilistic systems to implementing their first quantum algorithms.

As a mentor for **QBronze184**, I’ll be supporting a new cohort of students, researchers, and professionals through:

* **Basics of Classical Systems:** Probabilistic bits and coin-flipping games.
* **The Quantum Leap:** Introducing the Hadamard operator, superposition, and measurement.
* **Quantum Protocols:** Guiding them through the "magic" of Superdense Coding and Quantum Teleportation.
* **Grover’s Search:** Helping them implement one of the most famous algorithms in the quantum world.
