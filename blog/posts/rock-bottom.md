### July 14, 2025 | 7:29 AM

They say everything in life is a blessing, a lesson, and a gift. But sometimes I'm not too sure.

Be it school, work, or life in general. There are some days when you just sit back and ask... what went wrong?

Sometimes people say that finding true happiness is in finding joy in the little things. But why does it feel that every little thing causes pain and more pain?

They say money is the root of all evil but they don't talk about the evil without money.

Living in a place that dims your hopes, strips you of rights and being in debt, you will always ask one question. How will you get out of it?

They say once you hit rock bottom, the only way is up, but what if you toiled every day just to get to where you were, only for a word, a sentence, a remark, a life experience to wash up all your hard work.

There is a difference between the people who look back and move forward, and those who look back only to realize the distance was only just a step.

As for me, I am no aristocrat, I am no noble, but my words, my experiences are valuable, they are the only thing I add to my name, the only thing that adds "Lady" to a peasant.

I have a question to ask you? How did you move forward even when life seemed too hard? What are your motivations? Your incentive to push harder, because as for now, I need a hand.

Yours,

— **Lady Anuelle**
